ABOUT

Meagan Dietert is a 21-year-old student passionate about creative media and design. Currently, she is studying Creative Media and Fashion Merchandising at Texas Tech University and is currently based in Lubbock, Texas.

She is a multidisciplinary artist who enjoys working in photography, videography, digital composition, and textile art. Her work is largely centered around self-expression, a desire to connect with others, and the inherently intertwined nature of politics and art.
